üìö Definition Lists

Aperture
The opening in a lens that controls how much light enters the camera, measured in f-stops (f/1.4, f/2.8, f/5.6, etc.)
ISO
The camera sensor's sensitivity to light; higher ISO values allow shooting in darker conditions but may introduce noise
Shutter Speed
The length of time the camera's shutter remains open, controlling motion blur and exposure
Depth of Field
The area of the image that appears sharp and in focus, controlled primarily by aperture setting
